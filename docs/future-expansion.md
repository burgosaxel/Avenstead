# Avenstead Future Expansion Notes

This MVP is intentionally scoped around recurring household life-admin.

## Planned extensions

- Notifications: move reminder and weekly-summary scheduling into Cloud Functions or cron-backed jobs.
- Shared invites: convert placeholder household member invites into real email invitation flows.
- OCR: extract names, expiration dates, and document types from uploads.
- AI suggestions: suggest starter items, cleanup opportunities, and weak spots in household coverage.
- Email parsing: convert emailed bills, forms, and confirmations into suggested items or linked documents.
- Military-specific module: add deployment, PCS, spouse admin, and benefits workflows as a separate module or product variant later.

## Guardrails

- Keep the household life-admin focus intact.
- Avoid turning the product into generic project management or a full budgeting suite.
- Preserve the documents-to-actions relationship as a core differentiator.
